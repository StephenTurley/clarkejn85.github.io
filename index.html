Queens of the Stone Age, "QOTSA" is a rock band that originated from the Palm Desert, California music scene. Formed in 1996, QOTSA was created from the culmination of The lead guitarist and eventual lead vocalist, Josh Homme's effort to continue playing after his original band Kyuss split up the year prior.
