Queens of the Stone Age

Queens of the Stone Age (QOTSA) is a rock band originating from the Palm Desert, California area. Formed in 1996, QOTSA was created from the culmination of lead guitarist, and eventual lead vocalist, Josh Homme's effort to continue playing after the breakup of his original band Kyuss the year prior.
